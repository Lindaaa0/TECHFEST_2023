ABOUT US

Background

TECHFEST 2023 is a competition event initiated by HIMTI Binus University with the aim of providing a forum for creativity and competition for all students in Indonesia. This year, TECHFEST has the theme "Think Futuristic and Deliver Growth" and will be held at Binus University @Kemanggisan - Anggrek Campus.

History

The Technology Festival (TECHFEST) was first held in Malang under the name “HIMTI Festival” in 2021 online due to the Covid-19 pandemic. However, this did not dampen the enthusiasm of the participants to be able to participate and enliven the series of HIMTI Festival 2021 activities.

Goals

By organizing TECHFEST 2023, the abilities of students throughout Indonesia will be tested with various examples of cases found in each competition and bringing up new innovations. Gain new experience and knowledge from existing seminars, as well as add new connections, and be able to collaborate between organizations

About Logo

TECHFEST logo has its own meaning. The bulb represents thinking in an innovative and futuristic way, and the seed included in the center of the bulb represents growth. These two elements symbolize the theme and goal of TECHFEST 2023, namely "Think Futuristic and Deliver Growth" Using a combination of bright colors gives the impression of a fun and lively event.
